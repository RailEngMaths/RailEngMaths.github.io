Rail Engineering Mathematics

Sheffield Hallam University

This website is a repository of the material from the first year maths module: Rail Engineering Mathematics/Rail Engineering Mathematics for Apprentices. Having access to this site will allow you to revisit the material from the module throughout the duration of your studies (generally you lose access to the Blackboard sites as you progress through the years).


This site will be particulary useful before starting the level 6 module Applied Engineering Mathematics/Applied Engineering Mathematics for Apprentices. By no means are you expected to revise all 24 topics, but feel free to revisit any topic you wish. However, we do strongly advise that you revise these topics prior to the starting the level 6 module, as they are used heavily within the module. We will do some light revision.


The 24 links below contain the lecture slides, tutorial worksheets and solutions and videos. Be aware that topics and materials are from 2022, so may differ slightly from the year that you took the module.
